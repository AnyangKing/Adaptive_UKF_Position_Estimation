# Official source notes

Official source checked on 2026-07-12:

- Sensors Instructions for Authors: https://www.mdpi.com/journal/sensors/instructions

## Points used in this dry run

- Sensors encourages use of Word or LaTeX templates, but accepts free-format submission.
- Free-format submissions still require core manuscript components, including author information, abstract, keywords, introduction, materials and methods, results, conclusions, figures/tables with captions, funding information, author contributions, conflict of interest and related ethics statements.
- Research manuscripts should include front matter, manuscript sections, and back matter such as supplementary materials, author contributions, funding, data availability, acknowledgments, conflicts of interest, and references.
- Sensors emphasizes reproducibility and full experimental details.
- Data availability statements are required, and authors are encouraged to share research data, code, algorithms, protocols, and related material where possible.
- Equations should be editable rather than inserted as image-only content.
