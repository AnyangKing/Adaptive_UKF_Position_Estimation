## Safe defaults if the professor is unavailable

- Do not invent author contributions.
- Do not invent funding.
- Do not state "no conflicts" without human confirmation.
- Do not promise public data/code release until the repository contents have been cleaned.
- Use placeholders marked "human-author decision required" rather than final-looking fake statements.
