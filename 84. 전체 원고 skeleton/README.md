## 원고의 최종 중심축

> Static long-range shallow-water USBL localization is limited by carrier-locked coherent multipath DOA bias after direct-path gating. Ping-to-ping carrier agility whitens that residual and reduces 600 m static RMSE, while moving and faster quasi-static cases reveal motion/geometry-dependent applicability boundaries.
